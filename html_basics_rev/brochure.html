Prescription Upload

If the order contains any medicine which requires medical prescription , the prescription is to be uploaded and order will be approved by the pharmacist soon.

PICK UP

For Pick Up orders, the order must be collected by the user from the store within one hour of ordering. Failure to do so will automatically cancel the order.

Standard Delivery

For this, the order will be delivered to the respective hostels within one hour of ordering and standard delivery charges will be applied.

Urgent Delivery

For urgent deliveries, the order will be delivered in 5-10 minutes of ordering and standard delivery charges+ extra urgent delivery charges will be appied
